President's Message

When I was elected President of The Android Club AIIT, over a year ago, I wasn’t quite sure what to expect. Up until that day, I had never been president of any other club or institute. I have learned so much in the past months, and I want to tell you that it is truly gratifying and satisfying to work with such smart, talented and energetic faculty members, the core team of our club and amazingly generous, kind and supportive club members. We have accomplished many goals together, both large and small, and all of them important.

- Bharat(Android)
