About Us

Cascade is a club which teaches or provide information about web development among the students.

The major focus of Amity Cascade club is on student development as well as integrating stident with latest technical and research skills to make them equipped for a bright future.

Our vision is to improve the quality and global relevance of web development and research in India with related benefits to educators around the world.
