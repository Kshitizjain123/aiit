AIIT Department

Information Technology (IT) is the buzz word today and has made tall strides in every field of life. There is no soul untouched with the miracles of Information technology. IT has grown in the last few decades and the amount of influence it has on everyday lives is mind-boggling. The advent of computers has digitized several aspects in our day-to-day lives. It is amalgamation of computational, cognitive and social aspects that have made pentration of Information Technology, the impavt of which is greatly felt both by individuals and by organizations.

Computer Applications courses provide an apt platform for a marriage between IT skills and managerial insight. BCA and MCA course not only teach the building blocks of computers and its architecture but also integrate this learning with highly industry-specific and relevant knowledge of real world computer applications. They teach the students their real world applications. And this is what makes these courses highly industry relevant and acceptable.

Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan

President, Amity Institute of Information Technology Founder President, RBEF Ritnanad Balved Education Foundation (RBEF) (The Foundation of Amity Institutions and the sponsoring body of Amity Universities)

Amity Institute of Information technology (AIIT), Noida, integral part of Amity University Uttar Pradesh, is a centre of excellence for quality education in Information Technology with special focus on emerging trends. AIIT is a CISCO Regional Networking Academy since March 2001, now known as CISCO Instruction Training Center (ITC) and Network Academy. AIIT is providing this course as a Value Addition Course to external students as well as the students of MCA, M.Sc.(NT&M), Ph.D(IT), BCA, B.Sc. (IT), BCA+MCA (dual) and BCA (Evening). AIIT also has a tie-up with CISCO and Intersystems Pvt. Ltd. and it is the first Institute in India to offer InterSystems CacheCampus Program to foster knowledge about Cache among the student community.<.br>

Amity University has collaboration with EMC2, Sun MicroSystems, Oracle , SAP, Infosys, etc. Being a part of the University, the students of AIIT have the opportunity to avail access to the courses offered by these organizations.

I am confident that all the students of AIIT, possessing both theoretical and practical knowledge and having all the virtues and traits required to become a successfull professional in today's competitive world, through their hard work would prove to be an asset to any organization they would join.
